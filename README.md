# Tokopedia Purchase Conversion Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project is a business case study that explores opportunities to improve purchase conversion on Tokopedia by analyzing customer behavior throughout the purchasing journey.

---

## Business Problem

Although product views are increasing, purchase conversion remains low.

Many users:

- Browse products
- Save products to Wishlist
- Add products to Cart

However, only a small number complete the purchase.

---

## Objectives

- Understand customer purchasing behavior
- Identify the biggest conversion drop-off
- Explore opportunities to improve purchase conversion
- Provide business recommendations

---

## Business Questions

- Why do users abandon purchases?
- Why is Wishlist usage relatively low?
- Which stage has the biggest conversion loss?
- How can Tokopedia encourage more users to complete purchases?

---

## Methodology

- Funnel Analysis
- Customer Behavior Analysis
- Competitor Analysis
- Business Recommendation

---

## Key Findings

- Product views are relatively high.
- Wishlist engagement exists but awareness remains low.
- The biggest drop-off occurs before checkout.
- Customers often wait for discounts or compare prices across platforms.

---

## Proposed Solutions

- Improve Wishlist visibility
- Add Checkout button directly from Wishlist
- Almost-out-of-stock notifications to urge purchase on cart
- Flash Sale countdown notifications
- Limited-time voucher notifications

---

## Expected Business Impact

- Increase purchase conversion
- Improve Wishlist engagement
- Reduce purchase drop-off
- Enhance customer experience
