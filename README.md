# 🛒 Tokopedia Purchase Conversion Analysis
Business Case Study | Product Analytics | Customer Behavior Analysis

> How can Tokopedia increase purchase conversion by improving Wishlist, Cart, and Checkout experience?

## 📌 Project Overview
This business case explores why purchase conversion remains low despite increasing product views.
The objective is to identify customer pain points, analyze user behavior, benchmark competitors, and propose product improvements that can increase purchase conversion.
---

## 💼 Business Problem
### Key Findings
- Product views continue increasing
- Wishlist usage is relatively decent
- Add-to-cart is very low
- Purchase conversion remains low

The biggest drop-off occurs before checkout.
<p align="center">
<img src="images/2%20business_problem.PNG" width="850">
</p>

## 👥 User Research
To validate assumptions, interviews were conducted with several marketplace users.
Main findings:

- Many users rarely use Wishlist.
- Users prefer Cart for faster purchases.
- Wishlist is perceived as difficult to find.
- Wishlist is mainly used to save products for later.

<p align="center">
<img src="images/3%20analysis.PNG" width="850">
</p>

## 🔍 Root Cause Analysis
Based on data analysis and qualitative interviews, several root causes were identified:

- Wishlist visibility is low.
- No purchase reminder.
- No urgency after adding products to Wishlist and Cart.
- Checkout requires unnecessary steps on Wishlist feature.

<p align="center">
<img src="images/3a%20analysis.PNG" width="850">
</p>

## 📱 Product Analysis
Current user flow introduces unnecessary friction.
Wishlist users must first move products into Cart before purchasing.
This additional step increases purchase abandonment.
